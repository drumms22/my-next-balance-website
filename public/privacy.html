Privacy policy

My Next Balance (“the app”) — last updated April 2026.

What the app does with your information

The app runs in your web browser. The financial information you enter (for example income, expenses, and configuration) is stored locally on your device using your browser’s storage (such as localStorage) so your settings can persist between visits. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not transmit your budget data to our own database for storage.

Hosting and technical data

The site is served over the internet. Our hosting provider may process standard technical information involved in delivering web pages (for example network requests). That processing is governed by the provider’s policies.

Changes

We may update this page when the app or hosting changes. The “last updated” note at the top will change when we do.
